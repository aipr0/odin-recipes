The Burger

The succulent burger, a balanced helping of nature's blessings...

A single dish providing a balanced helping of nature's blessings: meat, grain and vegetable.

When taste falls short, so does our gratitude to nature. God gave us MSG, and by God we'll use it!

Ingredients

  • Burger meat
  • Bun
  • Onions
  • Salad
  • Ketchup
  • Flavourings...

Method

  1. Tenderise the meat; shape it.
  2. Cook the onions. Cook the burger.
  3. As you cook the burger, add the all important... "flavourings". I will leave it up to your discretion on what chemicals to use.
  4. Grill the buns too if you like.
  5. Slap the patty on the bun, load with onions and salad and ketchup, slap the lid on... and enjoy! And enjoy and enjoy and enjoy and enjoy and...
