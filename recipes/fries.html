The French Fry

Perhaps the most evil thing you could do with the hearty potato

Of all the things. Creamy mashed potato. Crispy jacket potatoes. Loaded potato skins. Bubble and squeak. My granddad's potato fritters. It had to be fries huh?

Alright whatever, they taste okay I guess. Easy to cook too, if a bit greasy...

Ingredients

  • Fries
  • Oil
  • Some sauce or dip hopefully

Method

  1. Heat the oil.
  2. Throw (but gently) a few fries in to test the oil. You'll know if it's ready
  3. Dump the rest in, fry until golden brown (or however you like them I guess).
  4. Strain and drain, dump over some paper towel. Eat as soon as they're not dangerously hot. Wow, that was hard.
