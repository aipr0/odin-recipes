The Hotdog

The delicious hotdog, a staple of the modern peasant...

Everyone knows and loves the hotdog. Not many can resist the aroma of spiced (reclaimed) meat wedged inside of a thick bun.

Today you'll learn how to recreate the hotdog in your very own domicile, lucky you.

Ingredients

  • Wiener
  • Bun
  • Onions
  • Ketchup
  • French mustard

Method

  1. First make the onions. Chop the onions as you like, heat some oil, fry the chopped onions.
  2. Grill the wieners, or boil them, however you like it.
  3. Load the bun with the wiener, onions, and the relish.
  4. The most important step: Enjoy!
